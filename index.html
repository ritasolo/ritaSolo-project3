Mercurian ☿️

Mind / Communication / Intellect / Reason / Language

  • you are the the entrepreneur, innovator & storyteller.
  • sarcastic, quick witted and fast learners
  • + curious, communicative, and adaptable
  • - indecisive, critical, and anxious

Venusian ♀

Beauty / Harmony / Balance / Love

  • you appreciate the pleasure of the five senses
  • care about sophistication, beauty, elegance and true love
  • + sense of the aesthetic, charming, cheerful
  • - superficial, shallow, childish

Earthling ⊕

Strength / Dependability / Pragmatism / Common Sense

  • you are responsible, diligent, pragmatic, & have a no-nonsense approach to life
  • commited and methodical, but also open minded and accepting
  • + patient, tolerant, dedicated
  • - rigid, overplanning, materialistic

Martian ♂

Passion / Energy / Courage / Vitality

  • you are enterprising, encouraging, powerful
  • you may be competitive, athletic and/or ambitious
  • + adventurous, sincere, straightforward
  • - reckless, aggressive, egocentric

Jupitor ♃

Generous / Enthusiastic / Discerning / Philosophical

  • you are often in positions of authority and knowledge
  • you value growth, scientific insight and prosperity
  • + humane, fair, dutiful, optimistic
  • - risktaking, wasteful, greedy

Saturnian ♄

Patience / Sophistication / Minimalism / Justice

  • you are often stoic, thoughtful and analytic
  • you appreciate quality, luxury, elegance (but also simplicity)
  • + thorough, possess integrity, tenacious
  • - pessimistic, stubborn, depressive

Uranian ⛢

Altruism / Original / Creative / Independent

  • you are innovative, a revolutionary and very sensitive
  • drawn to the occult and paranormal
  • + balanced, inspired, unique, in tune
  • - moody, hypersensitive, distractable

Neptonian ♆

Spirituality / Magic / Empathy / Intuition

  • drawn to non-material realm
  • deeply in tune with emotions of self and others
  • + humble, compassionate, emotionally wise
  • - escapist, melodramatic, lethargic

Plutonian ♇

Depth / Creative Inspiration / Self Analysis / Truth

  • you value the study of self, others, the subconscious and healing
  • dedicated to discovering truth and fighting for it
  • + revolutionary, practically intelligent, deep and intense interests
  • - possessive, jealous, power hungry
